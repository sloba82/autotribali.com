How to apply?
There are several ways to apply sticker. On this occasion, we'll show you one of ways to apply sticker.
Click on picture to enlarge
This is how sticker looks like when it is ready to apply
It consists of a background paper, sticker and transfer film.
 
 
Sticker set to the desired location that you previously cleaned well.
Attach a label with masking tape, through the middle of the sticker.
 
 
Carefully separate the sticker from the background paper
Peels off up to half or stickers.
 
Now you can remove the background paper.
 
 
 
 
After you have removed the background paper apply half of the sticker.
For applying sticker usesqueegee, you can also use any object with a straight edge (credit card).
 
 
This how it should look like half of the label applyed to the surface.
 
 
 
After removing the second half of the background paper, you can repeat the procedure and apply second half of the sticker
 
 
 
Ako ste sve odradili kako treba ovako izleda nalepnica sa koje je potrebno još samo ukloniti transfer foliju.
Transfer folija se odlepljuje vrlo lako.
